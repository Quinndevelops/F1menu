F1 Manager 2024

About

F1 Manager 24 in Shortcuts uses HTML for its sleek look and your own Files app, so you don't have to install anything. It's a great and easy way to save all your stats automatically. Get your favorite drivers on tour team and see if you can win the constructors cup!!!
